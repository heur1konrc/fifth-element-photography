**What is 3:2 Aspect Ratio?**
- **Mathematical Ratio:** 1.5:1 (width is 1.5 times the height)
- **Common Source:** Most DSLR cameras (Canon, Nikon, Sony, etc.)
- **Example Dimensions:** 6000x4000, 4500x3000, 3000x2000, 1800x1200

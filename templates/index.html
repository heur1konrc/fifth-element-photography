The Art of the Unseen



Born under the wide skies of South Central Wisconsin, my life has been a canvas of different hues and disciplines. A musician's ear for harmony and feel for rhythm, a teacher's patience, and a business owner's drive have all shaped the artist and the person I am today. Yet, it is through the lens of a camera that my spirit finds its truest voice. My camera is not just a tool; it's a silent partner on a constant expedition, always in search of the next story to tell.

I chase the unseen, the fleeting moments hidden within the commonplace. My eye is captivated by the elegant geometry and stark lines of architectural design, and I find a profound truth in the timeless beauty of black and white. I am just as comfortable in the hushed stillness of my studio as I am out in the elements, where the pursuit of the perfect shot might mean getting soaked or muddy. I have learned to embrace the quiet hours of waiting, when a subject may never appear, because the simple act of being present in nature is a gift for the soul.

My greatest joy lies not only in my own creations but in the act of collaborating. If you have a dream, a vision that yearns to be captured, let's embark on that journey together. Every image in my portfolio is available for purchase, and your support through tips and donations helps sustain this creative expedition, allowing me to continue to seek and capture these moments.
Rick
